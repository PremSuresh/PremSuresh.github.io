Magic Wrap

Leakage arrestor/Anti-Corrosion/Pipe reinforcer

Magic-Wrap® is a fiberglass pre-impregnated composite that is activated by salt or freshwater. Packaged in a hermetically sealed foil pouch, it is ready to use and does not require any measuring or mixing. This system, when used with the appropriate primary sealant, enables you to repair and reinforce virtually any diameter pipe in minutes. Magic-Wrap® not only restores a pipe to its original psi rating, but actually can make the repair area stronger than the original pipe, reducing downtime, saving in parts and labor. With an initial setting time of only 30 minutes (75°F/24°C), Magic-Wrap® is excellent on copper, steel, SS, PVC, FRP, clay, concrete, rubber, and more. It is safe to use, nontoxic, noncombustible, and odorless. Magic-Wrap is a versatile solution for industries where quick-action pipe repair can be critical.

PSI Epoxy Putty Sticks

Repair anything/rebuild anything/restore anything

PSI’s specialty epoxy putty sticks, epoxy putty tapes, epoxy putty bars, epoxy industrial pastes, and epoxy potting compounds offer a wide range of easy-to-use epoxy products for do-it-yourself and industrial use. Each is formulated to deliver optimum performance in a specific type of application. Some are fast-cure for quick, strong emergency repairs; others are slower cure for reconstruction, molding and sculpting. Some have NSF Standard 61 certification for use with drinking water. All are user-friendly, low-hazard products for tough, permanent repairs. All have excellent resistance to a wide range of chemicals and solvents.
